********************To Install*************************************

To install, unzip and place 'EasyTransferToslink' folder into your 'C:\Users\{user name}\Documents\Arduino\libraries' folder or '{Arduino IDE path}\hardware\libraries" or {Arduino IDE path}\libraries" directory. 

Restart the Arduino IDE, look for the Library under "Sketch" -> "Import Library". You can also try the examples by finding them
under "File" -> "Examples" -> "EasyTransferToslink".

All uses of the library are in the example sketchs.
